ScreenPlay- Your Personal On-Stage Smartphone Simulator!


ScreenPlay is your ticket to realistic and engaging smartphone scenes on stage. This ultimate rehearsal support application for theatre performances is designed to simulate common smartphone interactions - from phone calls and emails to checking the screen, providing a nuanced acting experience.

In contemporary theatre, scenes often involve characters reacting to receiving a text message, answering a phone call, or simply looking at their smartphone screens. ScreenPlay makes these interactions easy to practice and perfect. With our app, you can play phone ring sounds, simulate incoming calls and text messages, and even facilitate the sending of faux emails.

Here's what ScreenPlay offers:

  • Phone Ring Simulator: Choose from a variety of common ringtones and adjust their volume to fit your scene perfectly.
  • Call and Message Simulation: Practice your lines with simulated incoming calls and text messages. You can customize the caller ID, message content, and timing for an authentic rehearsal experience.
  • Email Simulation: Prepare for scenes involving email notifications. Set up fake email senders, subjects, and contents for a realistic acting experience.
  • Screen Reaction: Elevate your performance by simulating scenarios that require reacting to smartphone screen content. You dictate the content that your character reacts to, providing depth and authenticity to your scenes.

ScreenPlay is the key to mastering smartphone interactions in your scenes. Don't let technological elements disrupt your flow – download ScreenPlay now and enhance your rehearsal process!
